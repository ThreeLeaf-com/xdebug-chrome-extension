Privacy Policy

Effective Date:

This privacy policy applies to the Xdebug Chrome Extension (the "Extension").

Data Collection

The Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data. All configuration and settings are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API.

Analytics and Tracking

The Extension does not use any analytics, telemetry, or tracking services. No usage data is sent to any server.

Permissions

The Extension requests only the minimum permissions required to function (such as cookie and storage access). These are used solely for enabling Xdebug features and saving your preferences.

Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use or connect to any third-party services.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us.
